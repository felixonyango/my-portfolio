About Me

I’m a web developer who juggles both front-end and back-end development, meaning I’m the one who makes websites look good and keeps them from crashing like an old Windows PC. My specialty? Creating sites so smooth, even your grandma could navigate them without calling for tech support. I also fully embrace AI because, let’s be real, why do all the hard work when I’ve got a digital sidekick? AI helps me code faster, smarter, and probably better than I ever could at 2 a.m. while running on caffeine fumes. Basically, I let the robots handle the complex stuff while I sit back and act like I totally had it all figured out. If you want a website that’s fast, user-friendly, and powered by a combo of human creativity and robot efficiency, I’m your developer. I’ll build it like I’m racing against a deadline, because let’s face it, I probably am!
